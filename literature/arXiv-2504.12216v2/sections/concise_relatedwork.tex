\section{Related Works}
\vspace{-2mm}
Due to space constraint, we provide a detailed related works discussion in Appendix~\ref{sec:related}.

\textbf{Diffusion Language Models.} Diffusion models, successful in visual domains \citep{songscore, ho2020denoising}, faced challenges in language due to text's discrete nature, initially tackled by modeling continuous diffusion on textual latents \citep{austin2021structured, gulrajani2023likelihood}. Masked diffusion emerged as an effective discrete variant \citep{austin2021structured, sahoo2024simple, shi2024simplified, ou2024your, nie2024scaling}, scaled notably in DiffuLLaMA \citep{gong2025scaling}, which initialized with pretrained LLaMA weights. Recent works explored chain-of-thought reasoning \citep{yediffusion, ye2024beyond}, block-based generation \citep{arriola2025block}, and large-scale competitive performance in LLaDA \citep{nie2025largelanguagediffusionmodels} and Dream \citep{dream2025}. However, reinforcement learning (RL) enhancement remains unexplored; we present the first demonstration using policy gradients for large diffusion language models. \textbf{Improving Reasoning Abilities of LLMs through SFT and RL.} Reasoning improvements in LLMs involve supervised finetuning (SFT) with high-quality reasoning datasets \citep{yu2023metamath, numina_math_datasets, pasteropenwebmath} or curated reasoning demonstrations \citep{ye2025limoreasoning, muennighoff2025s1, zhou2023lima}. However, RL approaches \citep{chu2025sft} generalize better, especially with methods like GRPO \citep{guo2025deepseek, shao2024deepseekmath}, facilitating advantage estimation without critic models. Advanced reasoning via RL alone was shown by DeepSeek-R1-Zero \citep{guo2025deepseek}, whose reasoning traces can be used to distill smaller-model, such as OpenThoughts \citep{openthoughts} and OpenR1-Math\footnote{\url{https://huggingface.co/datasets/open-r1/OpenR1-Math-220k}}. Prior RL work in discrete diffusion models \citep{zekri2025fine} employed concrete score matching and applied to smaller scale models, whereas our method specifically applies to large masked dLLMs with efficient masking-based policy gradients, integrating both SFT and RL.
